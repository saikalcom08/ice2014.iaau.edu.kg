Location and Travel

How will I Get to Bishkek from Turkey?

There are direct flights between Turkey and Kyrgyzstan from Istanbul Ataturk Airport to Bishkek Manas Airport by Turkish Airlines (THY) every day. Also, Pegasus Airlines has scheduled flights to Bishkek from Turkey four days a week.


Is there any access to Bishkek from the Neighbouring Countries?

From the neighbouring countries of Kyrgyzstan, only Kazakhstan (Almaty) has available access road to Bishkek. To take this, firstly you need to get to Almaty from Istanbul by airlines and afterwards from Almaty to Bishkek by using motorway or airlines. Turkish Airlines has scheduled flights to Almaty every day. Also, Pegasus Airlines has scheduled flights four days a week from Istanbul to Almaty. It takes approximately 3.5 hours to get to Bishkek by road from Almaty, and it takes approximately 45 minutes by airlines. Visa is not required when taking the arrivals over Kazakhstan. The taxi fare is $ 50 from Kazakhstan (Almaty) to Bishkek.


How will I get to the City Centre after arriving in Bishkek Airport?

Please inform us by E-mail or telephone about your date of departure from Istanbul and arrival time to Bishkek. Then, you will be picked up by our staff from the Airport.


What Shall I do in the case of a mishap?

If you do not have any contact number in the case of a mishap, you can get a taxi from Bishkek airport to the city centre which is 35 km from Bishkek. The taxi fare is around 500 som ($10). You can exchange currency at the airport.
