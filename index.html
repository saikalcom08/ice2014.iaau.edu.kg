W elcome to the web site of the 6th International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'14) organized by Faculty of Economics and Administrative Sciences of:

  • International Ataturk Alatoo University (Kyrgyzstan),
  • Kyrgyzstan-Turkey Manas University (Kyrgyzstan),
  • Suleyman Demirel University (Kazakhstan),
  • Celal Bayar University (Turkey).

6th International Congress on Entrepreneurship is going to be held in
Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan from April 24 to April 26 , 2014.

  • 1st International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'06) was held on May 25-27 2006 in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan.
  • 2nd International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'08) was held on May 07-10 2008 in Bishkek,Kyrgyzstan.
  • 3rd International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'10) was held on May 17-19 2010 in Bishkek,Kyrgyzstan.
  • 4th International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'12) was held on May 2-4 2012 in Manisa, Turkey.
  • 5th International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'13) was held on May 17-19 2010 in Almaty,Kazakhstan.

The 6th International Congress on Entrepreneurship (ICE'14)will be the assembling place for academicians and business professionals from all over the world.

The main purposes of the 6th International Congress on Entrepreneurship are to attract attention to the importance of business in order to stimulate the entrepreneurship in Turkic countries; to outline the problem spheres of entrepreneurship; to provide an opportunity for academicians and business professionals to come together and to interact with each other.

Participation of professionals from business world is warmly welcome in ICE'14. Their contribution is possible with following options:
- Case study,
- Success story,
- Paper.

The language of the congress is Turkish, Kyrgyz, English and Russian.
